TYPES OF CYBER ATTACKS

A cyber attack happens when cyber criminals try to gain illegal access to electronic data stored on a computer or a network. The intent might be theft of valuable data like hospital records etc. Cyber attacks can target individuals, groups, organizations, or governments.
