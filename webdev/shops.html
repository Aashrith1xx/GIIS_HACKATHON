Shops robbed as people find an excuse due to the alien invasion

As fear grips the world due to the impending alien invasion, some individuals are taking advantage of the chaos and using it as an excuse to loot shops. With law enforcement focused on the extraterrestrial threat, these opportunistic criminals are finding the perfect opportunity to carry out their thefts, leaving shop owners facing significant losses. The situation calls for unity and vigilance to protect vulnerable businesses during these trying times.
