Millionares rush to spend all their money amid alien invasion, fearing the worst.

As news of an impending alien invasion spreads, millionaires around the world are gripped by fear and uncertainty. In a desperate bid to safeguard their riches and prepare for the unknown, they are engaging in frantic spending sprees. Luxury items, expensive properties, and unconventional ventures are being pursued as they seek a sense of security in the face of the potential worst. Some are also choosing to make charitable donations, leaving behind a different kind of legacy as humanity prepares for the unprecedented threat ahead. The rush to spend wealth during this time highlights the profound psychological impact of the impending catastrophe on even the wealthiest individuals.
